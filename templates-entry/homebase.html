    Why Standard Based Grading?

    Focus on Understanding
    Shifts the focus from just getting good grades (numbers) to really understanding the material. It helps in identifying specific areas that need improvement
    Consistency Across Classes
    Offers a more consistent way of measuring student performance across different classes, ensuring fairness and comparability in assessments
    Continuous Improvement
    Focuses on ongoing progress. It encourages students to continuously work on their skills and understanding, rather than just aiming for high scores on single tests or assignments
